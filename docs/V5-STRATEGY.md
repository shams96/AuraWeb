## Language Directives for Codebase

### Remove Immediately
- All age references: Adolescent, Young Adult, Mature, "13-19", "20-29", "30-49", "50+"
- Tier labels: "T1 Teen", "T2 Twenties", "T3 Thirties+", "T4 Mature+"
- Badge text: "Age-Defying"
- Any "consumer" prefix in B2C product display names

### Replace With
- Protocol names: "Preservation Protocol", "Refinement Protocol", "Restoration Protocol", "Transformation Protocol"
- Skin Intelligence™ language throughout
- Performance-led descriptions (not age-led)
